What and Why

We live in a world where the internet is overloaded with graphic-heavy websites, GUI and emphasis is put more on designing than on legibility and viability. The Problem? All these "features" make it difficult for people with Dyslexia, Visual Impairment and Learning Disabilities.

We at Theia, provide a solution to this problem. Theia has the power to change any website you look at into something you can read. Theia can change the appearance of the website to suit your needs.

Easily Customised

Easily customise Theia to suit your needs. Take advantage of the custom themes or use our pre-defined themes to bring your webpage to life.

Audio Preview

Everytime you click on Theia, it gives you a short audio preview of what is on the webpage that you currently are on. No need to enable Theia.
